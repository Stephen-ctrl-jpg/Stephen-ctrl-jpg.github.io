Bachelor of Computer Application

The Bachelor of Computer Science program at Don Bosco College is designed to provide students with a robust foundation in computer science principles, programming languages, and cutting-edge technologies. The pedagogy is structured to balance theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience, ensuring that students not only understand the core concepts but can apply them in real-world scenarios.

The department at Don Bosco College is distinguished by its unique practices, including hackathons, industry collaborations, and a strong emphasis on problem-solving. Students have the opportunity to participate in coding competitions, internships, and collaborative research projects, fostering a spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship. Graduates of the Bachelor of Computer Science program at Don Bosco College can explore diverse career prospects, ranging from software development and artificial intelligence to cybersecurity and data analysis.
